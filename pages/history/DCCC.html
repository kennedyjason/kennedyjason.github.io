Associate in Science

Associate Degree from Davidson County Community College

During my time at Davidson County Community College, I was mainly studying general education because I was not sure what I wanted to study in university. I have always been interested in science, so my main focus was Chemistry, Biology, and Math. It was during my first semester of Chemistry that I found a subject that I would like to continue studying. The Professor at the time was an easy going guy who liked to tell jokes, and would always go off on tangents during his lectures, but he made the topic interesting. I would stay after class, and often visited his office to speak with him about studying Chemistry at University. However, it was not to be as I soon began to explore the world of computer programming. I immediately realised I had an affinity for it, and began to study it in my spare time. By the time I had finished my degree I had a much greater interest in Computers than I did in Chemistry, and thus my decision was made.
