CodeLinc

CodeLinc 4.0 - Hosted by Lincoln Financial

CodeLinc is an annual twenty-four hour programming competition, hosted by Lincoln Financial in Greensboro North Carolina. The challenge that year was to design a website or app that would help local food banks connect with their consumers, as well as assisting people who need help to find it. My groupd consisted of three other computer science majors from App State, and myself. We set out to build a website from scratch, we didn't want to use a template or framework. We all wanted to challenge ourselves into learning something new.

It didn't work out so well in terms of results, but we acheived our personal goals of learning new things. I myself was put in charge of handling the user interface, and never having used html or css, our website ended up looking dated. One team commented that it looked straight out of Windows XP. However, as far as I am concerned the night was a success as the four of us grew closer and together we created a website that would have helped those in need... in the 1990's.
